description=[h2]? Current Features[/h2]
description=[list]
description=[*][b]? Core Functions[/b]: Pee and poop as part of survival.
description=[*][b]? GUI Stats[/b]: Track bladder and bowel needs in a new bathroom tab.
description=[*][b]? Moodles[/b]: Urge indicators and soiled clothing effects.
description=[*][b]? Surface Support[/b]: Use toilets, urinals, outhouses, or the ground.
description=[*][b]? Containers[/b]: Pee in bottles or jars.
description=[*][b]? Clothing Effects[/b]: Textures, moodles, and penalties for soiling.
description=[*][b]? Urine Puddles & ? Feces[/b]: Physical impacts of your bodily functions.
description=[*][b]?? Urgency Hiccups[/b]: Random animations, sounds, and accident chance when urge is high.
description=[*][b]? New Traits[/b]: Traits that affect bladder and bowel capacity.
